# DOSBRE Homepage Layout Proposal

## 1. Hero Section
- **Logo & Slogan**: DOSBRE LTD — Ease your business process
- **Tagline**: "A digital platform for real estate and construction management."
- **Call to Action**: Buttons for "Explore Projects", "Meet Lumi", "Request Demo"
- **Visual**: Modern, professional hero image or animation

## 2. Lumi AI Assistant
- **Floating Lumi Chat**: Always available in the corner
- **Intro**: "Ask Lumi about DOSBRE, projects, or platform features."

## 3. About DOSBRE
- **What is DOSBRE?**: Short, bold summary of the business idea
- **Key Features**: Icons or cards for Property, Construction Projects, Document Management, Collaboration, Security

## 4. Projects & Case Studies
- **Featured Projects**: Cards for Stockmann, DB Schenker, Jätkäsaaren maanalaiset tilat (with images, short description, and link to full page)
- **Downloadable Materials**: Project PDFs or presentations

## 5. Platform Features
- **Property Management**: Legal, Financing, Sales, Services
- **Construction Project Management**: Designers, Contractors, Authorities, Plan Management
- **Document Management**: Centralized, secure, transparent
- **Integrations**: e-signature, maps, payments

## 6. Market & Vision
- **Market Opportunity**: Short stats/infographic about the real estate/construction market
- **Vision**: "One platform for all — from Finland to the world."

## 7. Team & Contact
- **Team**: Founder, developers, industry experts
- **Contact Form**: For demo requests, investor interest, or feedback

## 8. Footer
- **Links**: About, Projects, Contact, Privacy Policy
- **Socials**: LinkedIn, etc.

---

## Visual Style
- Black/gold/white color scheme (luxury, clarity, trust)
- Modern, clean, professional fonts
- Responsive for desktop and mobile
